About the app

Med Reminder helps you track medications, schedule dose alerts, and mark doses as taken. Smart meal-aware scheduling and stock alerts are included.

Medical disclaimer: This app is a reminder tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow instructions from your doctor or pharmacist.
