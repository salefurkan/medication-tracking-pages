Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

Med Reminder (“the App”) is developed by Furkan Şale. We designed the App so your medication schedules and related data stay on your device using Apple’s SwiftData. We do not operate a user account system and we do not sell your data.

Data we store on your device

  • Medication names, dosages, schedules, and stock counts you enter
  • Dose completion history and streak counters
  • Local notification identifiers required to deliver reminders

Data we do not collect

  • No account registration or email collection in the App
  • No cross-app tracking (no App Tracking Transparency prompt)
  • No analytics SDKs transmitting personal data to our servers in the current version

Notifications

The App uses Apple’s local notification APIs. Notification permission is requested on first launch. You can disable notifications in iOS Settings at any time.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

You can delete all data by removing the App from your device. You may also delete individual medications inside the App.

Contact

Questions: Support page or email support@example.com (replace with your production address before App Store submission).
