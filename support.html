Med Reminder — Medication Tracker

FAQ

Notifications do not appear

Check Settings → Notifications → Med Reminder. Ensure alerts are allowed. iOS limits pending notifications to 64 per app.

How do I delete a medication?

Swipe left on a medication in the list, or use the row context menu → Delete.

Is my data backed up?

Data is stored locally. Use iCloud device backup if you want schedules included in your phone backup.
